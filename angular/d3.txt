| Concept        | What it does                            | When it runs      |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| **Projection** | Converts *virtual layout* → *pixels*    | **Before render** |
| **Zoom**       | Scales & pans *already-rendered pixels* | **After render**  |

*/
/*	
	RAW DATA
   ↓
VIRTUAL LAYOUT (vx, vy)
   ↓
VIRTUAL BOUNDS
   ↓
PROJECTION (pxPerVirtual)
   ↓
PIXEL POSITIONS (x, y)
   ↓
RENDER
   ↓
ZOOM (camera)
*/
