|	RTRIM LTRIM											|
|														|
+-------------------------------------------------------+

	REASON

		LTRIM() and RTRIM() only remove ASCII space (CHAR(32)),
		
		NBSP IS CHAR(160) another characters 9 - tab, 10 - cr, 13 - lf
		

	How to detect the problem
	
		SELECT UNICODE(RIGHT('ברכה לוקשינסקי ', 1))	
	
		SELECT LTRIM(RTRIM(REPLACE('ברכה לוקשינסקי ', CHAR(160), ' ')))
